SUIE is here to serve the Raleigh-Durham-Chapel Hill Community!


SUIE has been assiting immigrants to this region, both documented and undocumented, for nearly 10 years. We are highly visible in the community and advocate for equal rights for immigrants to this country. Our group is comprised of immigrants themselves, so we understand, firsthand, the experience and struggle you may be facing. We regularly host events to benefit the community such as awareness nights, fundraising dinners, contacting local officials on behalf of individuals with language barriers, free consultations with immigration attorneys, as well as providing general advice to those in need of help obtaining documentation.
